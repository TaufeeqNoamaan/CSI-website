Welcome to CSI MJCET! Celebrating a decade of innovation, we're the proud hosts of the annual hackathon, "Hack Revolution," a breeding ground for groundbreaking ideas. Dive into the world of technology with our hands-on workshops, designed to give you practical skills that matter. Meet our community of tech talent, including Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors (MLSA) and the first GitHub Campus Expert of our college, making CSI MJCET a hub of innovation. Whether you're a coding enthusiast or tech explorer, join us at CSI MJCET to shape the future together!

About Us

CSI MJCET, the student chapter, was founded by our esteemed faculty coordinator, Zainuddin Naveed Sir. We are a dynamic community dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and learning.

Our activities include organizing a variety of events, hands-on workshops, and collaborative projects. These initiatives aim to upskill our members, providing them with valuable experiences and knowledge in the field of technology.
