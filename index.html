About Us

CSI MJCET, the student chapter, was founded by our esteemed faculty coordinator, Zainuddin Naveed Sir. We are a dynamic community dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and learning. Our activities include organizing a variety of events, hands-on workshops, and collaborative projects. These initiatives aim to upskill our members, providing them with valuable experiences and knowledge in the field of technology.
